By the numbers

A few facts worth remembering.

Bullying and cyberbullying affect a lot of young people, and the impact can reach emotions, schoolwork, friendships, and daily safety.

Bullied at school 0%

About 1 in 5 students reported being bullied, according to NCES 2022 data.

Online or by text 0%

Among students ages 12 to 18 who were bullied during school, 21.6% said it also happened online or by text.

Told a trusted adult 0%

Many students do reach out for help, but the number also shows plenty may still be carrying it alone.
